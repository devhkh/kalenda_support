Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add or edit an event?

To add an event:

1. Long press on the date where you want to add an event, and the event creation screen will appear.
2. Enter the event details, including title, time, and location (optional).
3. Tap Save to add it to your calendar.

To edit an event:

1. Tap on the event you want to modify.
2. Select Edit and make the necessary changes.
3. Tap Save to update the event.

How do I customize my calendar settings?

1. Go to the Calendar menu from the main screen.
2. Select the calendar you want to display and show only the desired one.
3. When you close the calendar selection screen, the selected calendar will automatically be displayed.

Is my data secure with EazyDay?

Yes, your data is completely secure with EazyDay:

- All calendar information is stored locally on your device unless you sync with a iCloud service.
- EazyDay does not share or sell your data to third parties.
- You can delete your data anytime through the Settings menu.
